From Grandma Vi!

2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup cocoa
2 cups white sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup milk
1 cup hot coffee
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs

1) Mix dry ingredients together
2) Add oil, milk, and coffee
3) Beat 1 - 2 minutes, medium speed
4) Add eggs & vanilla
5) beat again - batter will be thin
6) pour into 9X13 inch greased & floured pan
7) bake @ 350 for 35 - 40 minutes
8) Cool & frost.
