__cake__
1.5 cups brown sugar
0.5 cups butter
1 egg
1 cup sour milk (milk + a little vinegar)
1 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups flour
1.5 - 2 cups rhubarb
spread in greased pan

__topping__
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon (actually, I probably use a lot more than this)

1) spread cake into greased pan.
2) sprinkle topping on top
3) bake for around 45 minutes at 350
