Smoky Chicken, tomato, brown rice, and corn soup

2 tbsp olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
1 garlic clove - finely chopped
2 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp cumin
1 large can (28 ounces) peeled plum tomatoes with juices
1.5 cups leftover chicken (about 1/2 of a rotisserie chicken)
3 cups chicken broth
1 cup cooked brown rice (or other grain like quinoa / barley)
1 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup chopped cilantro leaves (stems are fine too)
1 heaping tbsp minced jalapeno
2 tbsp lime juice
a few spoonfuls of plain yogurt

1) Brown the onions in the oil in a large soup pan.  (10 min)
2) Stir in garlic (1 min)
3) Turn off heat & stir in cumin & paprika

4) Puree tomatoes (with juice) in food processor
5) Add chicken to food processor and pulse once or twice - chicken should be precut oto the size you want so that you don't have to pulse to much to get all chicken chopped!

6) Add puree to pot with onions
7) Add 3 cups of broth & jalapeno & simmer
8) Stir in rice, corn, 1/2 the cilantro leaves
9) When hot, remove from heat & add lime juice

10) Put a spoonful of yogurt into the center of each bowl of soup
11) Garnish with remaining cilantro.
