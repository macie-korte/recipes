__sauce mixture__
1/2 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/3 cup sherry wine
2 TBSP honey
1/4 tsp white pepper
1 TBSP sesame

__seasoning mixture__
some cooking oil
1TBSP minced garlic
1 TBSP minced ginger (or 1/2 TBSP ginger paste)

__thickener__
some water
some cornstarch

1)  stir the sauce mixture together in one bowl
2)  heat a pan, coat with oil, add the rest of the seasoning mix & stir fry for 15-30 seconds
3)  add the sauce mix, bring to a boild, cook for a minute
4)  thicken w/ dissolved corn starch
